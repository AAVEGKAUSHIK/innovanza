Gaming Maniac

    22nd & 23rd February, 2023     Venue - Online

“Be the last one standing!”

What is Gaming Maniac?

It is basically a fun event. Participants need to compete in online games i.e. BGMI/CODM and the winner shall be decided according to the criteria as specified further.



Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Students from any school (9th to 12th standard),recognized institution or college can participate in this event.
  • No external equipment is allowed.
  • Use of cheats/plug-ins is prohibited.
  • Camping is not allowed.
  • Decision of jury shall be final.

Point Scoring

  • Each match will consist of 16 teams competing in a given map. The official point system will be followed.
  • Kill Points: 1 point per kill.
  • 1st Position: 15 points.
  • 2nd Position: 12 points.
  • 3rd Position: 10 points.
  • 4th Position: 8 points.
  • 5th Position: 6 points.
  • 6th Position: 4 points.
  • 7 th Position: 2 points.
  • 8-12th Position: 1 point.
  • 13-16th Position: 0 points.
