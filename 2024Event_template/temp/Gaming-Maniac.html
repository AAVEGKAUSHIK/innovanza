It is basically a fun event. Participants need to compete in two online games, namely Mini Militia and COD-Mobile and the winner shall be decided according to the criteria as specified further.
roadmap

Flow of Event

Registration
Participants first need to register for the event and they will be informed about the events and rules.
Team Making
First Top 100 participants will selected after they compete in a match of Mini Militia. Those 100 participants will then be required to make a team of 4 among themselves. These 25 teams will be required to participate in a COD Battle Royale. Top 5 teams will be selected and then 20 participants will be required to make 4 teams of 5 players each. The semi-final and final will eventually be done in a TDM.
Prize Distribution
Top 3 among the final 4 teams left will be declared as winner and runner ups respectively.

Rules and Regulations

1. Students from any school (9th to 12th standard) or students of any recognized institution can participate in the event.

2. No external equipment is allowed.

3. Use of cheats/plug-ins is prohibited.

4. Camping is not allowed.

5. Decision of jury shall be final.

Judging Criteria

Participants first need to register for the event and they will be informed about the events and rules. Top 3 among the final 4 teams left will be declared as winner and runner ups respectively.
